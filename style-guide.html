Feedback: When I talked with my roommates they suggested that I try to differentiate my website and plan from what other business websites might be like. They also suggested that I make sure to outline why someone might want to pay for landscaping and other awn services.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to present the services of Sun Valley Services, a landscaping company. It will show customers how and what is possible to do with their property. This website needs to communicate the importance of different services offered as well as their price and benefit. It will be a place where potential customers can come and think about what they need or what is possible for their property. It will provide an environment where they can be inspired to finally commit and take their property to the next level and show how attainable their goals may be.

Audience Statement

Target customers are those who have disposable income to pay someone to maintain or improve their lawn or property. This will usually come from middle class and up. They often want to eliminate something to worry about like mowing the lawn or trimming plants. They want to feel better about their property and want it to look its best. They put their trust in our staff to deliver something that will always improve their yard rather than take away from it. Most customers will access this website by either laptop or mobile. Many of these people have busy lives and a good mobile site is a must.

Misson Statement

The website for Sun Valley Services will serve as a medium for people to come to know about landscaping and how to obtain the perfect lawn and property that they have always wanted. This company will serve those located in Eastern Washington. It will show customers the importance of various services and the benefits that come with them. We will be able to work with the customer to find exactly what they are looking for and make that happen. Our company takes pride in always delivering a better result than what we started with. When mistakes are made, all means will be taken to make things right.

Persona and Scenario Ideas

Personas:

James is a middle aged man and has a family. His motivation is to spend less time on yardwork and more time with his family and working. He wants his lawn and property to look amazing with little to no effort put in by him. He has a busy life with his family and work. This is why he wants to pay someone to take care of his property. He has a good amount of technical ability but gets confused at times with his phone.

Betty is an older woman who has been widowed. In her old age it is difficult to maintain or even care for her yard and property. She wants to keep her property looking great but does not have the energy or health to do so. Her goal is simply that, to perhaps replace her late husband by hiring us to do that work for her. Her technical ability is very limited and prefers calling or meeting in person.

Scenario:

Jane seeks out lawn services through our website. She is greeted by the most important services to her on the home page. Jane wants to know more about a specific service and can learn more about them on the services page. To gain ideas of what She wants to do at her Property, she looks at examples of finished projects and currently maintained properties. This shows the track record and quality of the services that can be delivered.
